Lemon Pig

Oh, fruit! · Just fruit, no pork.

Lemon Pig is a fruit identifier and pocket fruit encyclopedia for iPhone. Snap a photo — or type a name — and it identifies the fruit and serves a full profile: flavor notes, how to pick and store it, and recipes to cook with it.

Support

Questions, bug reports, fruit disputes, or recipe corrections — email sadief@gmail.com and a human will read it.

Good to know

  • Photo identification and profiles for fruits outside the built-in catalog are generated live by Claude, Anthropic's AI. Don't rely on it alone to judge whether a fruit is safe to eat.
  • Identification is capped at a fair number of scans per day so the free service stays free.
  • Lemon Pig has no accounts, no ads, and no tracking. See the privacy policy for the full story.

Credits

Catalog photography comes from Wikimedia Commons contributors and public-domain archives — every photo is credited inside the app under Photo Credits.
